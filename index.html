About Me

I am a graphic designer by trade from Caracas, Venezuela who moved to Australia in 2015. Since moving to Melbourne, I have fallen in love with the vast world of culinary arts. This new passion led me to become a chef. I enrolled at the William Angliss Institute in 2016 where I received my Certificate lII, Certificate lV and Diploma in Commercial Cookery. While I enjoyed my experience as a chef, my true passion lies in graphic design. I have continued working as a free-lancer. over the past 5 years. I am seeking the opportunity to further develop my knowledge and skills as a Graphic Designer. As a chef I learned to work efficiently in fast-paced and high pressure environments, multi-task, and work as part of a team. I know these skills will be beneficial in future roles as a Graphic Designer.
